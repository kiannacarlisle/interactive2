SNOW PIECE

Think that snow is
                           falling





























































































































think that snow is

                           falling all the time.



                                                      When you talk with a person, think



                                                                                                                 that snow is falling



                                                                                                                                                  Stop conversing when you think the



                                                                                                                                                                                                              person is covered by snow.
