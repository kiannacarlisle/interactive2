SNOW PIECE

Think that snow is

                        falling

Think that snow is

                      falling everywhere all the time


                                                       When you talk with a person,

                                                                                        think that snow is falling between you and the person.

                                                                                                                                                                                Stop conversing when you

                                                                                                                                                                                                                  think the other person is covered by snow

Yoko Ono summer 68
